What I've Made

Rising Edge

Rising Edge is a website for artists trying to gain traction, venues looking for performers, and people trying to find new music. The website aims to help artists find new listeners by providing listeners with powerful discovery features that promote both undiscovered and trending tracks. In addition, the website allows artist and venues to discover and contact one another in order to streamline the process of gig-finding for artists and venues alike.

The process of designing Rising Edge was conducted over 5 weeks in a usability and design course at UC San Diego. Our team, composed of myself and 5 other students, performed user testing, created personas and storyboards based on that user testing, and then prototyped the final website.

Assistant+

Siri extensions for jailbroken iOS devices

Assistant+ allows anyone to easily personalize and enhance Siri by creating custom responses and Activator listeners for Siri commands. Integrating Siri with Activator gives Siri unprecedented control over your device and allows for some exciting possibilities. Here are some examples:

  • • Use Siri in you car? You can create an Activator listener to turn off Wi-Fi, turn on Bluetooth, turn up the volume, and open Spotify when you say, “Start car mode”
  • • Set up any command you want to open an app or toggle a setting
  • • Using Activate Command you can send any command you want to a web server, or even send a Wake On Lan “magic packet” to your computer when you say, “Wake up my computer”
  • • Annoyed that Siri doesn’t dismiss itself? You can set up an Activator listener to dismiss Siri when you say, “Go away”

In addition, the triggers support wildcards so, for example, (.*)start car mode(.*) will match “Hey Siri start car mode please”, “Please start car mode”, “Start car mode Siri”, etc.

Leveraging the power of Activator means that the possibilities are endless, but sometimes Activator isn’t quite enough. That’s why Assistant+ also includes an easy-to-use plug-in framework. The framework handles all of the behind-the-scenes work, and allows developers to create plugins in a native Xcode environment without the need for Logos or any private frameworks. With the help of plugins nearly anything is possible: Spotify integration, controlling your computer, viewing RSS feeds, etc.

BizCards

iOS app for business card management

Inspired by the UC San Diego Mitek hackathon, BizCards leverages Mitek's mobile OCR technology and Parse's backend service to allow users to catalogue and store business cards to the cloud by taking a photo of them with their iOS device.

Using the same technology that powers mobile check deposits, BizCards makes it possible for you to collect the most important information from a business card by just taking a picture of it. Information like names, email addresses, and phone numbers are captured by the app and then stored both locally and in the cloud so that you can access the information from anywhere. This information can then be imported into your contacts or used through the app.
